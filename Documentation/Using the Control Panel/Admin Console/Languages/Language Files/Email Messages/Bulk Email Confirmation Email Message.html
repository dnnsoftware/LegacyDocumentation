Bulk Email Confirmation Email Message

The Bulk Email Confirmation email message is sent to the primary Administrator when a bulk email (newsletter) mail out is completed. To locate this resource file, use the Find option on your web browser and search for EMAIL_BulkMailConf_Subject.Text and EMAIL_BulkMailConf_Html_Body.Text.
