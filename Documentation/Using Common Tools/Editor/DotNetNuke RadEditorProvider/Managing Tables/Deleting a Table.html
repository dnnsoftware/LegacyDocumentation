Deleting a Table

How to delete a table from the DotNetNuke.RadEditorProvider for the RTE.

Option One:

  1. Click on the corner of the table to select it.
  2. Strike the Delete button on your keyboard.

Option Two:

  1. Click on the corner of the table to select it.
  2. Click on the corner of the table to select it. and select Delete Table from the drop down list.

Option Three:

  1. Place your cursor before or after the table.
  2. Strike either the Backspace or Delete button on your keyboard respectively.
