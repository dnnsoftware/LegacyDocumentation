Editing a Social Group

How to edit a social group using the Social Groups module. Social Groups can also be managed using the Admin > Security Roles modules.

  1. Navigate to a Social Groups module.
  2. Click on the name of the required Social Group.
  3. Click on the Edit Group link.
  4. On the Edit Group page, edit one or more fields as required. See "Adding a Social Group"
  5. Click the Save Group link.
