Approving Product Reviews

Catalog Managers can approve one or more store product reviews using the Store Admin module. Reviews can be submitted to the Store by all users; however they do not appear until they are authorized.

  1. On the Store Admin module, click the Reviews link.
  2. Recommended. At Status, select Not Approved.
  3. Optional. Filter reviews by Category and/or Product as required. See "Filtering Product Reviews" 
  4. Click the Edit link beside the required review.

  1. Optional. In the Comments text box, edit the comments if required.
  2. At Approved, to approve the review and display it on the product detail page.

  1. Click the Update link to approve this comment.
  2. Repeat Steps 3-7 to approve additional reviews.
