Completing Checkout - PayPal Payment

How to complete the checkout process when PayPal is the payment method for the Store.

  1. Complete See "Completing Checkout - Order Review". Once you complete this step, you will be located on the Payment tab of the Checkout page.
  2. Click the Confirm Order button to proceed to the PayPal website.

  1. Create a PayPal account (if you don't already have one) and then login and complete the transaction on PayPal. Once the order is complete you will receive confirmation via email.
