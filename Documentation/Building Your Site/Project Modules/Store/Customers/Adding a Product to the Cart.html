Adding a Product to the Cart

How to add a product to your shopping cart using the Store module.

  1. Go to the Product Detail page of the required product. See "Viewing Product Details"
  2. Optional. In the Quantity text box, edit the number of items required.
  3. Click the Add To Cart  button.

  1. The product has now been placed in your shopping basket and details of the item can be seen in the Store Mini Cart. The Store returns to the selected category allowing you to continue shopping.
