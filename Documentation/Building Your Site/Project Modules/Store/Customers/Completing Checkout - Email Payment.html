Completing Checkout - Email Payment

How to complete the checkout process when email is the payment method for the Store.

  1. Complete See "Completing Checkout - Order Review". Once you complete this step, you will be located on the Payment tab of the Checkout page.
  2. Click the Confirm Order button to complete this order. You will now receive confirmation via email.

  1. Review the details of the order.
  2. Optional. Click the Display Order link to view the full details of your order.

  1. This displays the order in detail.
