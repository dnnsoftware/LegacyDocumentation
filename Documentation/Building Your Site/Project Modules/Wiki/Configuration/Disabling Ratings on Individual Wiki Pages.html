Disabling Ratings on Individual Wiki Pages

How to disable rating from being added to one or more pages of the Wiki module. This task assumes ratings have been enabled for individual pages. This task can also be performed when adding a new page.

  1. Navigate to a page where you want to disable ratings. See "Navigating the Wiki"
  2. Click the Edit link.
  3. At Enable Page Ratings, to disable.
  4. Click the Save button.
  5. Repeat Steps 1-4 to disable ratings on other pages.
