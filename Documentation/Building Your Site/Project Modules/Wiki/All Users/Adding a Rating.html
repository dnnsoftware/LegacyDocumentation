Adding a Rating

How to rate the content of a Wiki page. Users are restricted to one rating per page which is tracked by computer using a cookie. Note: Ratings may be disabled on some or all pages.

  1. Navigate to the required page. See "Navigating the Wiki"
  2. In the Rating section, click inside one of the five radio boxes to select your rating from one to five where one is poor and five is great.

  1. Click the Rate Content link. Your rating is now added to the rating tally and a message reading "Thank you for rating this Page!" is displayed.
