Viewing Topic History

How to view the history of the changes made to a page in the Wiki module.

  1. Navigate to the required page. See "Navigating the Wiki"
  2. Click the View Topic History link. This displays the History for this page.

  1. In the Topic column, click on any version to view the content of that version.

  1. Click the << Back to return to the History.
  2. Repeat Steps 3-4 to view other versions.
