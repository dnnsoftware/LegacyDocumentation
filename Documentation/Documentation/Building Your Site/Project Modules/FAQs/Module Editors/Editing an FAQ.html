Editing an FAQ

How to edit a frequently asked question on the FAQs module.

Editing FAQ details or category

  1. Click the Edit button associated with the FAQ to be edited.
  2. Edit the one or more fields as required.
  3. Click the OK button to confirm.

Reordering FAQs

  1. Click the Up or Down button beside the FAQ to be edited to move the FAQ up or down one position, respectively,
  2. Repeat to continue reordering.
