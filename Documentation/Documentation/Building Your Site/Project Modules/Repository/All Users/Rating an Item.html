Rating an Item

How to rate an item in the Repository module. Note: Rating of items may not be available to all users.

  1. Locate the required item.
  2. At RATING, click on the Rating Stars  or Dashes . This displays the User Rating panel below the item. Note: If you are not authorized to add rating the User Rating panel is not displayed.
  3. Click inside a radio button to select your rating. Rating options are 0 - 10.
  4. Click the POST YOUR RATING button. Your rating is now added to the total tally.
