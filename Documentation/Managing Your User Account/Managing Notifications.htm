Managing Notifications

How to view and respond to notifications of events such as a site member choosing to follow your journal or sending you a friend request.

  1. Click the Check Notifications button. The number of new notifications, if any, is displayed here.
  2. You can now do any of the following:
    • On friend requests, you can choose to Accept or Dismiss each friend request.
    • Where a member has chosen to follow you, you can choose to Follow Back or Dismiss.
