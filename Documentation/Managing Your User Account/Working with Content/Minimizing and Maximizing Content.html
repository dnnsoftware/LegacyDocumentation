Minimizing and Maximizing Content

How to minimize or maximize module content. Note: This skin object is not used the default skin provided with DNN.

  • Click the Maximize button to show content.
  • Click the Minimize button to hide content. Only the title, header and footer are visible.

Tip: If you are logged in, these settings are remembered the next time you visit the site enabling you to customize the way you view different pages.
